About

Dr Mohammed MAIZA received his M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science from the University of Sciences and Technology of Oran, Oran, Algeria. He is currently a research scientist in the Computer Science Department at the University of Oran 1 Ahmed Ben Bella, Oran, Algeria. His research focuses on bioinformatics, computational biology, and machine learning, with an emphasis on high-dimensional gene selection, microarray data analysis, and hybrid evolutionary algorithms for cancer classification. He also works on robotics, computer vision, and autonomous vehicles.
